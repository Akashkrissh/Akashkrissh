## About

I build AI-powered security systems — combining machine learning, large language models, and structured incident response to automate threat detection at scale.

Final-year B.Tech student in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science at Karunya Institute of Technology and Sciences (GPA: 7.0/10, May 2026). My focus is on closing the gap between raw telemetry and actionable intelligence: designing pipelines that ingest, score, and explain security events without requiring an analyst in the loop for every alert.

I am particularly interested in how LLMs can augment security operations — not as a novelty, but as a practical layer over SIEM workflows and threat intelligence pipelines.
